New Company Paramount Skydance Corporation (Nasdaq: PSKY)

Combined Company Includes:

  • Paramount Pictures
  • CBS (broadcast network + CBS News)
  • Showtime
  • MTV, Nickelodeon, BET, Comedy Central
  • Paramount+ and Pluto TV
  • Skydance Film, TV, Animation, Gaming divisions

Key Changes Under Ellison:

  • Showtime/MTV Entertainment Studios ‚Üí Paramount Television Studios
  • $2 billion in planned cost savings (cuts expected)
  • Focus: franchises (Top Gun, Star Trek), sports, gaming, animation
  • Streaming films "not a priority" per Ellison
Impact on Documentaries: MTV Documentary Films is now in flux. The label will likely be "quiet" while the Ellison regime restructures priorities. Not a good time to pitch.

Disney+ / Hulu Consolidation

In Progress
Status Hulu app phasing out in 2026
What's Happening All Hulu content integrating into Disney+

Disney now owns 100% of Hulu after buying out Comcast's stake. The separate Hulu app will be retired, with all content moving to Disney+. This follows the pattern of Hulu replacing Star in international markets.
